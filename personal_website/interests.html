Interests and Hobbies

Music

Music has always been a big part of my life. It is more than just a nice beat or random words, there is comfort in knowing that someone can describe exactly how you are feeling in a rythmic 3 minutes. A song can hold years of culture and childhood memories that would have been forgotten otherwise. I truly and whole-heartedly believe music is another form of therapy!

Dance

I do not think that I have any regrets in life because I believe everything happens for a reason, but if I were to ever have one it would stopping my dance career. There was a feeling of content and pure bliss whenever I danced. I do not think I will ever stop loving dance. I remember the three years of middle school where I dedicated my entire being into dance, from Hip-hop to contemporary the feeling is the same.

Gym

Now I spend some of my free time in the gym. I am focused on weight trainning. I have to admit I am not as passionate about it as dance, but it comes very close. My goal is to be able to lift double my body weight with any muscle group. I have learned a lot through the process of getting serious in the gym regarding my body and nutrition.



Chess

BEWARE! I am just a novice in chess, but over the four months that I have started learning how to play, I have to admit progress has been made! It is a thought-provoking game that I advise anyone and everyone to play. You have to be able to keep the ultimate goal in mind (checkmate) while dodging any fatal attacks from your opponent and maintaining your offensive position. It is exhilarating!



Religion and Spirituality

I am not one to discuss religion or anything of the sort because it is a sensitive topic for many; however, the journey I have begun this year in becoming more in tune with myself and everything I believe to be true is something I cannot omit. It is a journey that I am no where near finished and it is not easy yet I am excited to experience every second of it.
